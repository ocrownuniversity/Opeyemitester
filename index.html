Shipping Policy

Shipping Policy

Last updated: January 2025

Shipping Coverage

Benim ships to all states across Nigeria. We partner with reputable logistics companies to ensure your orders arrive safely and on time.

Shipping Fees

  • Orders above ₦150,000: FREE shipping — no delivery fee applied.
  • Orders below ₦150,000: A flat delivery fee of ₦3,500 is added at checkout.
  • Delivery fees are calculated automatically based on your cart total.

Processing Time

Orders are processed within 1–2 business days after payment is confirmed. You will receive a WhatsApp notification when your order is shipped.

Estimated Delivery Times

  • Lagos: 1–2 business days
  • Abuja, Port Harcourt: 2–3 business days
  • Other states: 3–5 business days
  • Remote areas may take slightly longer.

Order Tracking

Once your order is shipped, you can track it using the Track Order feature in the app. You'll also receive WhatsApp updates at every status change.

Delivery Policy

Delivery Policy

Last updated: January 2025

Free Delivery Threshold

Orders with a total value of ₦150,000 or above qualify for free delivery anywhere in Nigeria. Orders below ₦150,000 attract a flat fee of ₦3,500.

Delivery Process

  • After placing your order, our team will confirm it within 24 hours.
  • You will receive a WhatsApp notification at every stage: Confirmed → Processing → Out for Delivery → Delivered.
  • Ensure your delivery address and phone number are accurate to avoid delays.

Failed Deliveries

If a delivery attempt fails (e.g., no one available to receive), our logistics partner will try again within 24 hours. After two failed attempts, the order may be returned and a re-delivery fee may apply.

Pay on Delivery

For Pay on Delivery orders, please have the exact amount ready in cash when the delivery agent arrives. Do not pay to any party claiming to be from Benim unless they show proper identification.

Damaged or Wrong Items

If you receive a damaged or wrong item, please contact us within 24 hours of delivery via WhatsApp at +234 916 803 1213 with photos. We will resolve the issue promptly.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Can't find your answer? Contact us — we're happy to help!

How do I place an order?
Browse products, add items to your cart, and click "Proceed to Checkout". You'll need to be signed in and have a saved delivery address. Choose your payment method and confirm your order.
When do I get free delivery?
Orders with a total value of ₦150,000 or above qualify for free delivery. Orders below ₦150,000 attract a flat delivery fee of ₦3,500, automatically added at checkout.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept Bank Transfer (Opay) and Pay on Delivery. Bank details: Opay | Adewole Aderonke Aishat | Account Number: 9168031213. Please use your Order ID as the transfer reference.
How do I track my order?
Go to Menu → Track Order and enter your Order ID or the phone number you used during checkout. You'll also receive automatic WhatsApp updates whenever your order status changes.
How long does delivery take?
Lagos: 1–2 business days. Abuja & Port Harcourt: 2–3 business days. Other states: 3–5 business days. Remote areas may take slightly longer.
Can I return a product?
Yes! You can return items within 7 days of delivery if they are defective, damaged, or incorrect. The item must be unused and in its original packaging. Contact us via WhatsApp to start the process.
Is my personal information safe?
Yes. We take your privacy seriously. Your data is stored securely using Google Firebase. We never sell your personal information to third parties. Read our Privacy Policy for details.
I made a Bank Transfer — how do I confirm my payment?
After transferring, send your payment receipt and Order ID to our WhatsApp: +234 916 803 1213. Our team will confirm your order within a few hours during business hours.
