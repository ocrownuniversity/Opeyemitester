About BENIM Trade

Nigeria's Premium Fashion Destination

BENIM is a modern Nigerian trading and fashion brand built on quality, trust, and value. We curate footwear and fashion essentials designed to match everyday style with a premium feel — without the premium price.

Our mission is to bridge the gap between affordability and style, delivering products that feel global but remain accessible. At BENIM, we are not just selling products — we are building a trusted lifestyle brand.

Why Choose BENIM?

  • ✅ Carefully curated products from trusted suppliers
  • ✅ Competitive pricing — no middleman markup
  • ✅ Reliable delivery across Nigeria
  • ✅ Responsive customer support via WhatsApp
  • ✅ 7-day return policy on all items

Delivery Policy

Last updated: 2024

Delivery Options

We offer two delivery options: Home Delivery and Pick-up Station. Choose what works best for you at checkout.

Delivery Timeframe

  • Ibadan & environs: 1–2 business days
  • Lagos, Abuja: 2–4 business days
  • Other states: 3–7 business days

Delivery Fees

Delivery fees vary by location and are calculated at checkout. Orders above ₦15,000 qualify for a delivery discount. Exact fee is communicated after order placement.

Order Processing

Orders are processed within 24 hours. You will receive a WhatsApp confirmation once your order is confirmed and dispatched.

Failed Delivery

If delivery fails due to incorrect address or unavailability, we will attempt re-delivery once at no extra cost. After that, additional charges may apply.

Return & Refund Policy

Last updated: 2024

Return Window

We accept returns within 7 days of delivery. Items must be in their original condition, unworn, and with original packaging.

Eligible Returns

  • Defective or damaged products
  • Wrong item delivered
  • Item significantly different from description

Non-Returnable Items

  • Items worn or used
  • Items without original packaging
  • Sale/clearance items (unless defective)

Refund Process

Approved refunds are processed within 3–5 business days. Refunds are made via bank transfer or Opay to the account you provide. Delivery fees are non-refundable.

How to Return

Contact us on WhatsApp (+234 916 803 1213) with your order ID, reason for return, and photos of the item. We'll guide you through the process.

Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2024

Information We Collect

We collect information you provide: name, email, phone, delivery address, and order history. We also collect basic usage data to improve our service.

How We Use Your Information

  • To process and deliver your orders
  • To send order updates via SMS/WhatsApp
  • To improve our products and services
  • To respond to your enquiries

Data Security

Your data is stored securely on Firebase (Google). We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

Your Rights

You may request deletion of your account and data at any time by contacting us on WhatsApp.

Terms & Conditions

Last updated: 2024

Acceptance of Terms

By using BENIM Trade website and placing orders, you agree to these terms and conditions.

Products & Pricing

All prices are in Nigerian Naira (₦). We reserve the right to change prices at any time. Prices shown at time of order placement are final for that order.

Order Acceptance

An order is confirmed only after we send a WhatsApp/SMS confirmation. We reserve the right to cancel orders due to stock unavailability or pricing errors.

Payments

We accept Pay on Delivery and Bank Transfer. For bank transfer, orders are dispatched only after payment confirmation.

Limitation of Liability

BENIM Trade's liability is limited to the value of the product ordered. We are not liable for delays caused by third-party logistics providers.
